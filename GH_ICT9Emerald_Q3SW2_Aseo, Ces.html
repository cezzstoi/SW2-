"The Little Rascals" is a comedy film about a group of neighborhood children who try to save their clubhouse and win a go-kart race, while learning valuable lessons about friendship and teamwork.
"Matilda" is a family comedy film about a gifted young girl who uses her telekinetic powers to overcome neglectful parents and a tyrannical principal, while finding solace in books and a kind-hearted teacher.
"Jigsaw" is a horror film and the eighth installment of the "Saw" franchise, where a new series of gruesome traps and puzzles appear, leading the police to believe that Jigsaw may not be dead. The film features a high body count and a dark and suspenseful atmosphere, typical of the franchise.
"Cruella" is a crime comedy film that tells the origin story of the iconic Disney villain, Cruella de Vil. The movie follows a young fashion designer who adopts the persona of Cruella and becomes a ruthless criminal. The film features a star-studded cast, stunning costumes, and a great soundtrack set in 1970s London.
"Pitch Perfect 2" is a musical comedy film that follows an all-female a cappella group, the Barden Bellas, as they compete in an international music competition, facing challenges both professional and personal. The film is known for its energetic musical performances and showcases the power of friendship and teamwork.
